Your Data Protection Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request restriction or objection to processing
  • Request data portability

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please send an email to:

privacy@dual.cat

Please include sufficient information to identify your account and clearly state your request.

Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, send an email from the address associated with your account with the subject:

“Account Deletion”
