Your Data Rights

You can exercise your data protection rights by sending an email to privacy@dual.cat.

Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email from the address associated with your account to privacy@dual.cat with the subject "Account Deletion". The body of the email should include:

  • The email associated with your Dual account
  • A clear statement of your request, for example: “I request the deletion of my Dual account.”
