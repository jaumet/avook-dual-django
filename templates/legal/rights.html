Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Object to processing
  • Request portability of your data
  • Request a restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact:

Email: privacy@audiovook.com

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email associated with your Audiovook / DUAL account
  • The specific right you wish to exercise

In case of account deletion, please state explicitly:
“I request the deletion of my Audiovook / DUAL account.”

You will receive a response within 30 days.
